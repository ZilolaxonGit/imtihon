2-Topshiriq: Trigonometrik Limitlar

20-misol
$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5\text{tg}\,7x}{\sqrt{4x+9}-3}$$
Yechish:
Maxrajni ratsional qilamiz: $$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5\text{tg}\,7x}{\sqrt{4x+9}-3} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{4x+9}+3}{\sqrt{4x+9}+3}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5\text{tg}\,7x \cdot (\sqrt{4x+9}+3)}{(4x+9)-9}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5\text{tg}\,7x \cdot (\sqrt{4x+9}+3)}{4x}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5 \cdot 7x \cdot (\sqrt{4x+9}+3)}{4x} \cdot \frac{\text{tg}\,7x}{7x}$$
Eslatma: $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\text{tg}\,ax}{ax} = 1$
$$= \frac{5 \cdot 7 \cdot (\sqrt{9}+3)}{4} \cdot 1 = \frac{35 \cdot 6}{4} = \frac{210}{4} = \frac{105}{2} = 52.5$$
Javob: $\frac{105}{2}$ yoki $52.5$
21-misol
$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5\sin 3x}{\sqrt{2x+45}-3\sqrt{5}}$$
Yechish:
Maxrajni ratsional qilamiz: $$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5\sin 3x}{\sqrt{2x+45}-3\sqrt{5}} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{2x+45}+3\sqrt{5}}{\sqrt{2x+45}+3\sqrt{5}}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5\sin 3x \cdot (\sqrt{2x+45}+3\sqrt{5})}{(2x+45)-45}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5\sin 3x \cdot (\sqrt{2x+45}+3\sqrt{5})}{2x}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{5 \cdot 3x \cdot (\sqrt{2x+45}+3\sqrt{5})}{2x} \cdot \frac{\sin 3x}{3x}$$
Eslatma: $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin ax}{ax} = 1$
$$= \frac{5 \cdot 3 \cdot (\sqrt{45}+3\sqrt{5})}{2} \cdot 1 = \frac{15 \cdot (3\sqrt{5}+3\sqrt{5})}{2} = \frac{15 \cdot 6\sqrt{5}}{2} = \frac{90\sqrt{5}}{2} = 45\sqrt{5}$$
Javob: $45\sqrt{5}$
22-misol
$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{6+5x}-\sqrt{6-11x}}{11\sin 5x}$$
Yechish:
Suratni ratsional qilamiz: $$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{6+5x}-\sqrt{6-11x}}{11\sin 5x} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{6+5x}+\sqrt{6-11x}}{\sqrt{6+5x}+\sqrt{6-11x}}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{(6+5x)-(6-11x)}{11\sin 5x \cdot (\sqrt{6+5x}+\sqrt{6-11x})}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{16x}{11\sin 5x \cdot (\sqrt{6+5x}+\sqrt{6-11x})}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{16x}{11 \cdot 5x \cdot (\sqrt{6+5x}+\sqrt{6-11x})} \cdot \frac{5x}{\sin 5x}$$
$$= \frac{16}{55 \cdot (\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{6})} \cdot 1 = \frac{16}{55 \cdot 2\sqrt{6}} = \frac{16}{110\sqrt{6}} = \frac{8}{55\sqrt{6}} = \frac{8\sqrt{6}}{330} = \frac{4\sqrt{6}}{165}$$
Javob: $\frac{4\sqrt{6}}{165}$
23-misol
$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{4\text{tg}\,4x}{\sqrt{x+16}-4}$$
Yechish:
Maxrajni ratsional qilamiz: $$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{4\text{tg}\,4x}{\sqrt{x+16}-4} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{x+16}+4}{\sqrt{x+16}+4}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{4\text{tg}\,4x \cdot (\sqrt{x+16}+4)}{(x+16)-16}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{4\text{tg}\,4x \cdot (\sqrt{x+16}+4)}{x}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{4 \cdot 4x \cdot (\sqrt{x+16}+4)}{x} \cdot \frac{\text{tg}\,4x}{4x}$$
$$= 16 \cdot (\sqrt{16}+4) \cdot 1 = 16 \cdot (4+4) = 16 \cdot 8 = 128$$
Javob: $128$

3-Topshiriq: Funksiyalarni Uzluksizlikka Tekshirish

20-misol
$$f(x) = \frac{5|x|}{x} \cdot (x+2) + 5$$
Yechish:
Funksiya $x = 0$ nuqtada aniqlanmagan, chunki maxrajda $x$ bor.
$x > 0$ da: $f(x) = \frac{5x}{x} \cdot (x+2) + 5 = 5(x+2) + 5 = 5x + 15$
$x < 0$ da: $f(x) = \frac{5(-x)}{x} \cdot (x+2) + 5 = -5(x+2) + 5 = -5x - 5$
Chap tomondan limit: $\lim_{x \to 0^-} f(x) = -5 \cdot 0 - 5 = -5$
O'ng tomondan limit: $\lim_{x \to 0^+} f(x) = 5 \cdot 0 + 15 = 15$
Chunki $\lim_{x \to 0^-} f(x) \neq \lim_{x \to 0^+} f(x)$, funksiya $x = 0$ da uzilishga ega.
Javob: $x = 0$ - I-tur uzilish nuqtasi (sakrash uzilishi). Chap limit = -5, O'ng limit = 15
21-misol
$$f(x) = \frac{2x-1}{|2x-1|} \cdot x - 4$$
Yechish:
Funksiya $2x - 1 = 0$, ya'ni $x = \frac{1}{2}$ nuqtada aniqlanmagan.
$x > \frac{1}{2}$ da: $f(x) = \frac{2x-1}{2x-1} \cdot x - 4 = x - 4$
$x < \frac{1}{2}$ da: $f(x) = \frac{2x-1}{-(2x-1)} \cdot x - 4 = -x - 4$
Chap tomondan limit: $\lim_{x \to \frac{1}{2}^-} f(x) = -\frac{1}{2} - 4 = -\frac{9}{2}$
O'ng tomondan limit: $\lim_{x \to \frac{1}{2}^+} f(x) = \frac{1}{2} - 4 = -\frac{7}{2}$
Javob: $x = \frac{1}{2}$ - I-tur uzilish nuqtasi (sakrash uzilishi). Chap limit = $-\frac{9}{2}$, O'ng limit = $-\frac{7}{2}$
22-misol
$$f(x) = \frac{|4x+3|}{4x+3} \cdot (x-11) + 7$$
Yechish:
Funksiya $4x + 3 = 0$, ya'ni $x = -\frac{3}{4}$ nuqtada aniqlanmagan.
$x > -\frac{3}{4}$ da: $f(x) = \frac{4x+3}{4x+3} \cdot (x-11) + 7 = x - 11 + 7 = x - 4$
$x < -\frac{3}{4}$ da: $f(x) = \frac{-(4x+3)}{4x+3} \cdot (x-11) + 7 = -(x-11) + 7 = -x + 18$
Chap tomondan limit: $\lim_{x \to -\frac{3}{4}^-} f(x) = -(-\frac{3}{4}) + 18 = \frac{3}{4} + 18 = \frac{75}{4}$
O'ng tomondan limit: $\lim_{x \to -\frac{3}{4}^+} f(x) = -\frac{3}{4} - 4 = -\frac{19}{4}$
Javob: $x = -\frac{3}{4}$ - I-tur uzilish nuqtasi (sakrash uzilishi). Chap limit = $\frac{75}{4}$, O'ng limit = $-\frac{19}{4}$
23-misol
$$f(x) = \frac{3|x-2|}{x-2} \cdot x - 15$$
Yechish:
Funksiya $x - 2 = 0$, ya'ni $x = 2$ nuqtada aniqlanmagan.
$x > 2$ da: $f(x) = \frac{3(x-2)}{x-2} \cdot x - 15 = 3x - 15$
$x < 2$ da: $f(x) = \frac{3(-(x-2))}{x-2} \cdot x - 15 = -3x - 15$
Chap tomondan limit: $\lim_{x \to 2^-} f(x) = -3 \cdot 2 - 15 = -21$
O'ng tomondan limit: $\lim_{x \to 2^+} f(x) = 3 \cdot 2 - 15 = -9$
Javob: $x = 2$ - I-tur uzilish nuqtasi (sakrash uzilishi). Chap limit = $-21$, O'ng limit = $-9$

6-Topshiriq: Integrallar

20-misol
$$\int (3x+7)e^{3x+5} dx$$
Yechish:
Bo'laklab integrallash formulasi: $\int u\,dv = uv - \int v\,du$
$u = 3x + 7$, $du = 3dx$
$dv = e^{3x+5}dx$, $v = \frac{1}{3}e^{3x+5}$
$$\int (3x+7)e^{3x+5} dx = (3x+7) \cdot \frac{1}{3}e^{3x+5} - \int \frac{1}{3}e^{3x+5} \cdot 3dx$$
$$= \frac{(3x+7)e^{3x+5}}{3} - \int e^{3x+5}dx$$
$$= \frac{(3x+7)e^{3x+5}}{3} - \frac{e^{3x+5}}{3} + C$$
$$= \frac{e^{3x+5}(3x+7-1)}{3} + C = \frac{e^{3x+5}(3x+6)}{3} + C$$
Javob: $\frac{e^{3x+5}(3x+6)}{3} + C$ yoki $e^{3x+5}(x+2) + C$
21-misol
$$\int 4xe^{3x+2} dx$$
Yechish:
$u = 4x$, $du = 4dx$
$dv = e^{3x+2}dx$, $v = \frac{1}{3}e^{3x+2}$
$$\int 4xe^{3x+2} dx = 4x \cdot \frac{1}{3}e^{3x+2} - \int \frac{1}{3}e^{3x+2} \cdot 4dx$$
$$= \frac{4xe^{3x+2}}{3} - \frac{4}{3}\int e^{3x+2}dx$$
$$= \frac{4xe^{3x+2}}{3} - \frac{4}{3} \cdot \frac{e^{3x+2}}{3} + C$$
$$= \frac{4xe^{3x+2}}{3} - \frac{4e^{3x+2}}{9} + C = \frac{e^{3x+2}(12x - 4)}{9} + C$$
Javob: $\frac{4e^{3x+2}(3x-1)}{9} + C$
22-misol
$$\int 9x^2 e^{2x} dx$$
Yechish:
Birinchi bo'laklab integrallash:
$u_1 = 9x^2$, $du_1 = 18x\,dx$
$dv_1 = e^{2x}dx$, $v_1 = \frac{1}{2}e^{2x}$
$$\int 9x^2 e^{2x} dx = 9x^2 \cdot \frac{1}{2}e^{2x} - \int \frac{1}{2}e^{2x} \cdot 18x\,dx$$
$$= \frac{9x^2 e^{2x}}{2} - 9\int xe^{2x}dx$$
Ikkinchi bo'laklab integrallash:
$u_2 = x$, $du_2 = dx$
$dv_2 = e^{2x}dx$, $v_2 = \frac{1}{2}e^{2x}$
$$9\int xe^{2x}dx = 9\left(x \cdot \frac{1}{2}e^{2x} - \int \frac{1}{2}e^{2x}dx\right)$$
$$= 9\left(\frac{xe^{2x}}{2} - \frac{e^{2x}}{4}\right) = \frac{9xe^{2x}}{2} - \frac{9e^{2x}}{4}$$
Birlashtirsak: $$\int 9x^2 e^{2x} dx = \frac{9x^2 e^{2x}}{2} - \frac{9xe^{2x}}{2} + \frac{9e^{2x}}{4} + C$$
Javob: $\frac{9e^{2x}(2x^2 - 2x + 1)}{4} + C$
23-misol
$$\int 5x \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}} dx$$
Yechish:
$u = 5x$, $du = 5dx$
$dv = 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}dx$, $v = \frac{3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{\frac{3}{2}\ln 3} = \frac{2 \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{3\ln 3}$
$$\int 5x \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}} dx = 5x \cdot \frac{2 \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{3\ln 3} - \int \frac{2 \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{3\ln 3} \cdot 5dx$$
$$= \frac{10x \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{3\ln 3} - \frac{10}{3\ln 3}\int 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}dx$$
$$= \frac{10x \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{3\ln 3} - \frac{10}{3\ln 3} \cdot \frac{2 \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{3\ln 3} + C$$
$$= \frac{10x \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{3\ln 3} - \frac{20 \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{9(\ln 3)^2} + C$$
Javob: $\frac{10 \cdot 3^{\frac{3x}{2}}}{3\ln 3}\left(x - \frac{2}{3\ln 3}\right) + C$

7-Topshiriq: Egri Chiziqlar bilan Chegaralangan Soha Yuzini Topish

20-misol
$$x = (y-5)^3, \quad x = 2y - 10$$
Yechish:
Kesishish nuqtalarini topamiz: $(y-5)^3 = 2y - 10$
$(y-5)^3 = 2(y-5)$
$(y-5)^3 - 2(y-5) = 0$
$(y-5)[(y-5)^2 - 2] = 0$
$y = 5$ yoki $(y-5)^2 = 2$, ya'ni $y = 5 \pm \sqrt{2}$
Yuz: $S = \int_{5-\sqrt{2}}^{5+\sqrt{2}} |2y - 10 - (y-5)^3| dy$
$y = 5 - \sqrt{2}$ dan $y = 5$ gacha: to'g'ri chiziq kubik egri chiziqdan yuqorida
$y = 5$ dan $y = 5 + \sqrt{2}$ gacha: kubik egri chiziq to'g'ri chiziqdan yuqorida
Simmetriya tufayli: $$S = 2\int_{5}^{5+\sqrt{2}} [(y-5)^3 - 2(y-5)] dy$$
$t = y - 5$ almashtirish: $S = 2\int_{0}^{\sqrt{2}} (t^3 - 2t) dt$
$$S = 2\left[\frac{t^4}{4} - t^2\right]_0^{\sqrt{2}} = 2\left(\frac{4}{4} - 2\right) = 2(-1) = -2$$
Absolyut qiymat: $S = 2$ (yuza musbat)
Javob: $S = 2$ kvadrat birlik
21-misol
$$y = (x-4)^2, \quad y^2 = x - 4$$
Yechish:
Kesishish nuqtalarini topamiz. Birinchi tenglamadan: $y = (x-4)^2$
Ikkinchi tenglamaga qo'yamiz: $[(x-4)^2]^2 = x - 4$
$(x-4)^4 = x - 4$
$(x-4)^4 - (x-4) = 0$
$(x-4)[(x-4)^3 - 1] = 0$
$x = 4$ yoki $(x-4)^3 = 1$, ya'ni $x-4 = 1$, $x = 5$
$x = 4$ da: $y = 0$, $x = 5$ da: $y = 1$
$y^2 = x - 4$ dan: $x = y^2 + 4$
Yuz: $S = \int_{0}^{1} [(y^2 + 4) - (y + 4)^2] dy$
$$S = \int_{0}^{1} [y^2 + 4 - y^2 - 8y - 16] dy = \int_{0}^{1} (-8y - 12) dy$$
Wait, bu to'g'ri emas. Qayta ko'ramiz:
Parabola: $y = (x-4)^2$, ya'ni $x = 4 + \sqrt{y}$ (musbat shox)
Ikkinchi egri chiziq: $x = y^2 + 4$
Yuz: $S = \int_{0}^{1} [(y^2 + 4) - (4 + \sqrt{y})] dy = \int_{0}^{1} (y^2 - \sqrt{y}) dy$
$$S = \left[\frac{y^3}{3} - \frac{2y^{3/2}}{3}\right]_0^{1} = \frac{1}{3} - \frac{2}{3} = -\frac{1}{3}$$
Absolyut qiymat: $S = \frac{1}{3}$
Javob: $S = \frac{1}{3}$ kvadrat birlik
22-misol
$$y = (x+4)^2, \quad y^2 = x + 4$$
Yechish:
Bu 21-misolga o'xshash, faqat siljish bor.
Kesishish nuqtalari: $(x+4)^4 = x + 4$
$(x+4)[(x+4)^3 - 1] = 0$
$x = -4$ yoki $x + 4 = 1$, $x = -3$
$x = -4$ da: $y = 0$, $x = -3$ da: $y = 1$
Parabola: $x = -4 + \sqrt{y}$ (o'ng shox)
Ikkinchi egri chiziq: $x = y^2 - 4$
Yuz: $S = \int_{0}^{1} [(-4 + \sqrt{y}) - (y^2 - 4)] dy = \int_{0}^{1} (\sqrt{y} - y^2) dy$
$$S = \left[\frac{2y^{3/2}}{3} - \frac{y^3}{3}\right]_0^{1} = \frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{3} = \frac{1}{3}$$
Javob: $S = \frac{1}{3}$ kvadrat birlik
23-misol
$$y = x\sqrt{16-x^2}, \quad y = 0, \quad x = 0, \quad x = 4$$
Yechish:
Soha: $y = x\sqrt{16-x^2}$ egri chizig'i va koordinata o'qlari bilan chegaralangan, $x \in [0, 4]$
Yuz: $S = \int_{0}^{4} x\sqrt{16-x^2} dx$
Almashtirish: $t = 16 - x^2$, $dt = -2x\,dx$, $x\,dx = -\frac{dt}{2}$
$x = 0$ da: $t = 16$, $x = 4$ da: $t = 0$
$$S = \int_{16}^{0} \sqrt{t} \cdot \left(-\frac{1}{2}\right) dt = \frac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{16} \sqrt{t}\,dt$$
$$S = \frac{1}{2} \left[\frac{2t^{3/2}}{3}\right]_0^{16} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{2 \cdot 64}{3} = \frac{64}{3}$$
Javob: $S = \frac{64}{3}$ kvadrat birlik
